CPGE Grammar Bible — Chapter 17

Causative Structures (Have / Get Something Done)

Lesson

  • Have + object + past participle: We had the report revised.
  • Get + object + past participle: They got the article translated.
  • Have someone do something: The editor had the journalist rewrite the paragraph.
  • Get someone to do something: The minister got advisers to prepare a statement.

Common Uses

  • Delegation of tasks
  • Services performed by others
  • Administrative and professional contexts
  • Formal written English

Typical CPGE Examples

  • The government had the report rewritten.
  • The company got its strategy reviewed.
  • The newspaper had the article fact-checked.
  • The committee got experts to analyse the data.

Exercises

  1. Transform: Someone translated the report for the minister.
  2. Transform: Experts reviewed the proposal.
  3. Complete: The government had the law ______ (revise).
  4. Complete: The editor got the journalist ______ (rewrite) the article.
  5. Translate: Le gouvernement a fait réviser le rapport.

Answers

  1. The minister had the report translated.
  2. The committee had the proposal reviewed.
  3. revised
  4. to rewrite
  5. The government had the report revised.
