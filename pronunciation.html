Sound Lab · Priority repair

The TH workshop

The tongue must be visible. Air passes between the tongue and the upper teeth. The voiced sound /ð/ adds vibration in the throat.

/θ/

Build the sound into real speech

Touch your throat: /θ/ has no vibration; /ð/ does. Do not bite the tongue and do not pull it far out.

Four connected pathways

Build a voice that carries meaning

The progress stages move from perception to spontaneous use. Successful games and recordings update the early stages automatically; the final stage is an honest self-check after real use.
